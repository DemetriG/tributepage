SpongeBob SquarePants

The sponge that is square.

This is a tribute page to the life of SpongeBob.

  • 1986: Birth of a Square Sponge - On a sunny day in Bikini Bottom, the cheerful square sponge, SpongeBob SquarePants, was born. Instead of crying like regular babies, he giggled with bubbles.
  • 1992: Graduation from Mrs. Puff's Boating School - At the tender age of six, SpongeBob graduated from boating school and received his "Pretend License," complete with crayon drawings and glitter.
  • 1999: Jellyfishing Expedition - SpongeBob embarked on his first epic jellyfishing expedition with Patrick and Squidward. They accidentally discovered the "Jellysplosion," a rare phenomenon where jellyfish dance in harmony, creating a brilliant light show.
