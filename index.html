SpongeBob SquarePants

The sponge that is square.

This is a tribute page to the life of SpongeBob.

  • 1986: Birth of a Square Sponge - On a sunny day in Bikini Bottom, the cheerful square sponge, SpongeBob SquarePants, was born. Instead of crying like regular babies, he giggled with bubbles.
  • 1992: Graduation from Mrs. Puff's Boating School - At the tender age of six, SpongeBob graduated from boating school and received his "Pretend License," complete with crayon drawings and glitter.
  • 1999: Jellyfishing Expedition - SpongeBob embarked on his first epic jellyfishing expedition with Patrick and Squidward. They accidentally discovered the "Jellysplosion," a rare phenomenon where jellyfish dance in harmony, creating a brilliant light show.
  • 2002: Krusty Krab Dreams Come True - SpongeBob lands his dream job at the Krusty Krab. His infectious energy and love for krabby patties make him an instant hit with Mr. Krabs and Squidward, much to Squidward's chagrin.
  • 2004: Foiling Plankton's Plans - Plankton's continuous attempts to steal the Krabby Patty secret formula are thwarted by SpongeBob's quick thinking and unwavering dedication. The Krusty Krab remains the top spot for underwater fast food.
  • 2010: Superhero Adventures - SpongeBob and Patrick become unlikely superheroes, defending Bikini Bottom from evil. With their wacky powers and quirky teamwork, they give Mermaid Man and Barnacle Boy a run for their money.
  • 2015: Jellyfishing Champion - SpongeBob achieves legendary status as the jellyfishing champion of Bikini Bottom. His precision with the jellyfishing net is unmatched, and even Squidward secretly admires his skills.
  • 2020: Mayor SquarePants - SpongeBob takes on the role of Bikini Bottom's mayor. His policies include free Krabby Patties for everyone and mandatory bubble-blowing breaks. The city has never been happier.
  • 2023: Birthday Bash at the Krusty Krab - SpongeBob celebrates his 37th birthday with a massive party at the Krusty Krab. The whole Bikini Bottom turns out, and even Plankton shows up hoping to snag some leftover cake.
  • 2025: The Secret Ingredient - SpongeBob discovers a new Krabby Patty ingredient, elevating the burger to new heights of deliciousness. The Krusty Krab's popularity soars, leaving Plankton scratching his microscopic head in defeat.
  • And so, SpongeBob SquarePants continues to live his bubbly and adventurous life in Bikini Bottom, spreading joy and laughter to everyone around him.
